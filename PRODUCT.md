# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

General website visitors, prospects, and customers who need to understand how the site handles data, what terms apply, and what trust credentials Selim Solution can claim.

## Product Purpose

Provide clear public-facing legal and trust pages that support the marketing site, reduce friction for visitors, and reinforce credibility without distracting from the brand.

## Brand Personality

Secure, confident, premium.

## Anti-references

Generic legal pages that feel like copied boilerplate, dense walls of text, weak hierarchy, or trust pages that look like badge dumps.

## Design Principles

- Lead with clarity and credibility.
- Make legal content easy to scan and easy to trust.
- Keep the pages consistent with the main brand voice.
- Use structure and hierarchy to reduce reading friction.
- Treat trust signals as part of the brand, not decoration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast and readable line lengths. Keep headings semantic, content scannable, and interactions keyboard accessible. Respect reduced motion and avoid information that depends only on color.
